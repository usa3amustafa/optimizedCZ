foodtruck frizz

Inspired by my time in Los Angeles, where food trucks reign supreme, Food Truck Frizz is my original IP that tells the story of a shape-shifting alien who accidentally crash lands in Venice, CA and disguises his talking spaceship as a food truck. Little did he know, the foodtruck community would take him in as one of their own.
