ounce water

Alongside founder Theo Rossi, I had the privilege to develop the complete brand identity for Ounce Water from its logo, labels, packaging, to its marketing campaigns. The Ounce Water mission was to serve as a cultural statement to drink more water! The brand has reached hundreds of thousands of consumers domestically and has been in the hands of influencers like DJ Khaled, Ebro and was even featured on Marvel’s Luke Cage television series.
