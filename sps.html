standard print shop

What originally started as a project to “cut the middleman” in 2019 turned into a full service print business. Standard is my personal print shop where I design and print apparel to sell directly to my audience. I also print apparel for other entrepreneurs, influencers and businesses. Since it’s conception, Standard has continued to expand and now operates in both Los Angeles and Atlanta.
