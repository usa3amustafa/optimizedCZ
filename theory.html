theory

Actor Theo Rossi and Kim Coates AKA “Juice” and “Tig” stars of FX’s Sons of Anarchy, came together to create the THEory podcast. This podcast caters directly to SOA’s largest fanbase and my apparel designs kept the fans engaged. The signature mascot “The Plague Reaper” was an homage to the Grim Reaper on the series logo. On top of my apparel and illustration work, I also developed all of the branding for the podcast.
