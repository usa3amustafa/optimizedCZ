kip & gil

My original IP, Kip & Gil was selected as the 2016 winner of the MIA Pitch to the Industry Film Festival. Creating this show is what ultimately lead me to move to Los Angeles, which was the catalyst to fostering a large portion of my professional network. Kip & Gil was picked up by NBC Universal, but ultimately dropped due to unforeseen circumstances. Luckily, I retain all of the rights to this show and it’s still available to get picked up. This project stands as a true testament of what risk-taking can bring if you’re willing to take the leap.
