about

cezar arvelo

"Start where you are, Go where you are not"

Born in NYC, raised outside of Boston as a child and Orlando, FL as a teen, art and creativity has been my lifelong identity as I’ve never known anything different. After graduating college, my instincts took me back to NYC to begin the voyage of my now decade-long career. By means of pure hustle and ambition I’ve amassed a large and valuable network that I continue to foster today. As a loyal husband and father of two girls, I know what it takes to remain accountable while nurturing that for which I am responsible. I approach entrepreneurship with the same mindset and have managed to build a solid reputation for always delivering at the highest quality. I believe in cultivating the youth and I spend a lot of my time mentoring young creatives at the college level. Breaking generational curses through positive daily habits — like staying fit, eating healthy and working relentlessly on my craft —is what I live for
